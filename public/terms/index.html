Terms And Safety

Ace Domain is built for global conversation, communities, culture discovery, profile expression, and Ace AI support.

These rules help keep Ace Domain respectful, safe, and useful for global social discovery.

Acceptable Use

  • Meet people respectfully and protect personal boundaries.
  • Join communities around shared interests without spam or harassment.
  • Share posts, chats, and profile content responsibly.
  • Use Ace AI for creative help and discovery, not professional or emergency advice.

Prohibited Behavior

  • Harassment, bullying, hate, threats, stalking, or repeated unwanted contact.
  • Spam, scams, fraud, phishing, malware, or attempts to compromise accounts.
  • Impersonation, non-consensual sharing of private information, or illegal activity.
  • Sexual exploitation or harmful content involving minors.
  • Content that encourages self-harm, violence, or unsafe behavior.

Moderation And Reporting

Ace Domain provides in-app report and block controls through Settings and Global Safety. Reports can be sent to support@acedomain.app for review.

Ace AI Limits

Ace AI is separate from user-to-user chat. It may be unavailable when backend services, provider access, or quota are limited. Responses may be imperfect and should not be used as legal, medical, financial, emergency, or safety-critical advice.
