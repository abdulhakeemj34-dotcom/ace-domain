Privacy Policy Draft

This pre-release page explains the data Ace Domain may use as the app moves from demo/local mode toward live Supabase and Ace AI services.

Draft status: this page is preparation material for review. It is not final legal advice, not legal approval, and not app store approval.

Operator And Contact

Ace Domain is currently prepared under the placeholder operator name R.D.ACE / Ragnar D Ace Company until final legal details are confirmed.

Support contact placeholder: support@acedomain.app. This inbox must be created, monitored, and verified before public store submission.

Information Ace Domain May Collect

  • Account details such as email, username, authentication identity, and session state.
  • Profile details such as display name, avatar, bio, country, interests, and languages.
  • Social content such as posts, community activity, chat thread membership, and messages when live features are enabled.
  • Settings and preferences such as theme, accessibility, data saver, privacy, notification, and language choices.
  • Ace AI prompts and responses when the backend AI feature is enabled.
  • Diagnostics or analytics only if those services are added later and disclosed before release.

How Data May Be Used

  • Create accounts, restore sessions, and display profiles.
  • Save local and remote settings safely.
  • Support posts, communities, chats, notifications, discovery, and safety workflows.
  • Process Ace AI requests through a backend-only endpoint.
  • Respond to support, privacy, and account deletion requests.

Storage And Service Providers

Ace Domain may store local settings and demo state on the device. Live account, profile, settings, chat, feed, and community data may be stored in Supabase when configured.

Ace AI must keep provider keys backend-only. Depending on configuration, Ace AI may use Amazon Bedrock or OpenAI through a hosted backend endpoint.

User Rights And Deletion

Users should be able to ask for access, correction, privacy help, and account deletion. The final deletion workflow and retention rules must be confirmed before public release.

Open account deletion draft Pre-release

Security

Ace Domain should use secure transport and must not expose service-role keys, OpenAI keys, Bedrock keys, passwords, or private tokens in frontend code.
