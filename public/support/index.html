Support

Get help with Ace Domain accounts, safety, chats, communities, settings, privacy, account deletion, and Ace AI.

Contact support at support@acedomain.app. Do not send passwords, private recovery codes, API keys, Supabase keys, or payment details.

What Support Should Cover

  • Signup, login, rate-limit, and account access issues.
  • Profile, settings, theme, accessibility, and local/remote sync questions.
  • Feed, communities, chats, notifications, and demo fallback behavior.
  • Ace AI availability and backend provider issues.
  • Privacy, data access, account deletion, and safety reports.

How To Contact Support

Email support@acedomain.app with the issue type, device type, app version/build if known, and a short description of what happened.

Include the issue type, device type, app version/build if known, and a short description of what happened. For account requests, use the same email address connected to the Ace Domain account when possible.

Urgent Safety

Ace Domain is not an emergency service. For immediate danger, medical emergencies, legal emergencies, or safety-critical situations, users should contact local emergency services or trusted authorities.
