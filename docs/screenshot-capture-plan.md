# Screenshot Capture Plan

Date: July 23, 2026.

Screenshots must be captured from real tested builds before store submission. Do not use mock screenshots that misrepresent the submitted app.

## Required Phone Screens

Capture these after a successful Android/iOS build:

1. Welcome/Auth entry.
2. Home feed.
3. Chats inbox.
4. Chat room.
5. Communities.
6. Global Discovery.
7. Profile.
8. Settings.
9. Ace AI only if the backend is production-ready or clearly shown in a safe unavailable/demo state.

## Screenshot Rules

- Use the accepted black-first compact social layout.
- Do not show real passwords, tokens, private emails, or private chat content.
- Avoid showing test data that looks offensive, copyrighted, or unsafe.
- Show safety/settings/account deletion paths where useful.
- Use real app screens, not design mockups, for final store uploads.

## Store Notes

- Google Play phone screenshots are required for phone distribution.
- Apple screenshots should be captured from iPhone simulator/device sizes requested by App Store Connect at submission time.
- iOS screenshots require macOS/Xcode or a real iPhone capture workflow.
