# Terms And Community Safety Draft

Draft status: this is a readiness draft for Ace Domain. It is not final legal advice, not legal approval, and not app store approval. Final terms and safety policies must be reviewed before public release.

Public pre-release page draft: `/terms/`.

## Purpose

Ace Domain is a social app for global chats, communities, culture discovery, profile expression, and Ace AI support. The public version needs clear rules so users understand acceptable behavior and safety expectations.

## Acceptable Use

Users should use Ace Domain to:

- Meet people respectfully.
- Join communities around shared interests.
- Share posts and conversations safely.
- Respect privacy and personal boundaries.
- Follow community rules.
- Use Ace AI as a creative and discovery support tool, not as a substitute for professional advice.

## Prohibited Behavior

Ace Domain should prohibit:

- Harassment, bullying, intimidation, or stalking.
- Hate, abuse, threats, or dehumanizing behavior.
- Spam, scams, fraud, phishing, or misleading promotions.
- Illegal content or activity.
- Impersonation or false identity claims.
- Non-consensual sharing of private information.
- Sexual exploitation or harmful content involving minors.
- Malware, attempts to compromise accounts, or platform abuse.
- Content that encourages self-harm, violence, or unsafe behavior.
- Repeated unwanted messages or pressure.

## User-Generated Content

Users may be responsible for the content they post, send, or share. Ace Domain needs a final policy explaining:

- What content users own or license to the platform.
- What content may be removed.
- How reports and moderation decisions are handled.
- How deleted/suspended account content is treated.

## Moderation And Reporting Placeholder

Reporting, blocking, and moderation workflows are not fully production-live yet. Before public release, Ace Domain needs:

- A way to report users/content.
- A way to block or restrict unwanted contact.
- A moderation review process.
- Safety escalation instructions.
- Support contact for urgent abuse reports.

## Account Suspension Placeholder

Ace Domain may need the ability to restrict, suspend, or terminate accounts that violate final terms or safety rules. The final policy must describe the process and any appeal/support path.

## Community Rules Placeholder

Communities should have rules covering:

- Respectful conversation.
- No harassment or hate.
- No spam/scams.
- No illegal or unsafe content.
- Moderator/admin responsibilities.
- Removal of harmful content.

## AI Assistant Limitations

Ace AI may help with prompts, discovery ideas, profile copy, and social suggestions. Ace AI:

- May be unavailable when backend services or quota are limited.
- May produce imperfect or incorrect responses.
- Should not be used as legal, medical, financial, emergency, or safety-critical advice.
- Is separate from user-to-user chats.

## No Emergency Or Safety-Critical Reliance

Ace Domain is not an emergency service. Users should contact local emergency services or trusted authorities for urgent safety, medical, legal, or crisis situations.

## Future Reporting And Blocking Requirements

Before public release, Ace Domain should add or clearly document:

- Block user workflow.
- Report user/content workflow.
- Moderation review process.
- Account restriction process.
- Support response expectations.
- Data retention rules for safety reports.

## Final Review Required

Before publishing terms or community safety rules:

- Confirm operator/legal entity.
- Confirm support contact.
- Confirm moderation tools and workflow.
- Confirm account deletion and suspension policies.
- Confirm privacy policy consistency.
- Review against current Play Store and App Store requirements.
- Complete legal review outside this draft.
