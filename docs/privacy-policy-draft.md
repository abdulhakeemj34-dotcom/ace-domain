# Ace Domain Privacy Policy Draft

Draft status: this is a preparation draft for Ace Domain. It is not final legal advice, not legal approval, and not app store approval. A final privacy policy must be reviewed and published before public store submission.

Public pre-release page draft: `/privacy/`.

## Operator

Ace Domain is operated by **R.D.ACE / Ragnar D Ace Company** as a placeholder operator name until final business/legal details are confirmed.

Support contact placeholder: `support@acedomain.app` once created and monitored.

## Information Ace Domain May Collect

### Account Information

- Email address.
- Password handled by Supabase authentication.
- User ID/session data.
- Username or display name.

### Profile Information

- Avatar initials or profile image if added later.
- Bio.
- Country/region.
- Interests.
- Languages and global profile preferences.
- Profile style/personalization choices.

### Content And Social Activity

- Posts or feed content.
- Comments/replies if live posting is enabled.
- Likes, saves, shares, and other interactions if stored remotely.
- Community memberships and community posts.
- Chat thread membership and messages when live chat is active.
- Notification state and categories.

### Settings And Preferences

- Theme and appearance settings.
- Chat wallpaper and bubble settings.
- Accessibility settings.
- Data-saver preferences.
- Privacy/display preferences.
- Language/region preferences.

### Ace AI Messages

If Ace AI is enabled, messages sent to Ace AI may be processed by the Ace Domain backend and OpenAI. Ace AI is separate from user-to-user chat.

### Device Or App Diagnostics

Diagnostics, crash reports, analytics, and device metadata are not production-required in the current stage. If these are added later, the final privacy policy and store forms must be updated.

## Why Data May Be Used

Ace Domain may use data to:

- Create and manage accounts.
- Restore sessions.
- Display profiles.
- Save settings and preferences.
- Provide feed, community, chat, and notification experiences.
- Support global discovery and safety features.
- Process Ace AI prompts if the AI feature is enabled.
- Respond to support, privacy, and account deletion requests.
- Improve reliability and safety if analytics/diagnostics are added later.

## Where Data May Be Stored

### Local Device Storage

Ace Domain may store local settings, onboarding preferences, global profile preferences, safety settings, demo/local UI state, and session metadata on the user's device.

### Supabase Backend

Live account, profile, settings, posts, communities, chats, and notifications may be stored in Supabase when live backend features are configured and active.

## Third-Party Services

### Supabase

Supabase may be used for authentication, database storage, realtime chat, settings sync, and related backend features.

### OpenAI / Ace AI Backend

OpenAI may process Ace AI prompts when Ace AI is enabled through a backend/serverless endpoint. OpenAI keys must remain backend-only.

### Future Services

Future analytics, crash reporting, payment, or hosting providers may be added later. The privacy policy and store forms must be updated before those services are used in production.

## User Rights

Users should be able to:

- Request access to account/profile data.
- Correct profile or account information.
- Request deletion of their account.
- Ask privacy questions through support.
- Request help with local data/settings where possible.

The final support and deletion process must be published before store submission.

## Security

Ace Domain should use secure transport for backend communication and should not expose service-role keys or private API keys in frontend code. No security method can be guaranteed perfect, so final public language must be reviewed before publication.

## Account Deletion

Account deletion is not implemented as a destructive in-app flow yet. Before store submission, Ace Domain needs either an in-app deletion flow or a clear support-based account deletion request path.
